  • Manuel Kaufmann

    Manuel is the founder of "Argentina en Python", organizer of around 40 events to teach Python in more than 5 countries from Latam while he travels by car. This year was named "Python Ambassador" by the Python Software Foundation because of his work in the community. Nowadays, he works at Mozio building the future of ground transportation.
